<h1 align="center">Arts Council of Greater Greensboro Internship Project</h1>
<br />
<h2>Data Insights for Participant & Consultation Services Key Metric Data Analysis</h2>
-<b>Cleaned and transformed multi-year Artist Support Grant datasets using Python (Pandas, NumPy), standardizing demographic variables and removing null/zero values to create reliable datasets for strategic decision-making.</b>
<br />
<br />
-<b>Developed comprehensive Power BI dashboards visualizing 5+ years of funding trends, demographic breakdowns by race/gender/age, and award distribution patterns, enabling board members to track $187,872+ in total investments across 52 organizations</b>
<br />
<br />
-<b>Engineered predictive ARIMA time series forecasting models to project artist funding demand over the next 5 years, providing data-driven insights for budget planning and resource allocation</b>
<br />
<br />
-<b>Conducted cross-sectional analysis between Artist Support Grant and Professional Development Hub datasets, identifying conversion patterns where 23% of initially denied applicants successfully received grants after completing professional development programs</b>
